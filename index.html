About

FRED RACCOON – a new memeber in the realm inspired by the unforgettable story of Peanut the squirrel and Fred the raccoon. Fred’s journey, though cut short, resonated deeply with fans around the world, highlighting a powerful message of compassion, connection, and the importance of wildlife protection.

As a tribute to Fred’s legacy, FRED RACCOON aims to foster a community committed to wildlife preservation and awareness. Join us as we honor these beloved creatures and support efforts to protect animals everywhere. 🦝

How To Buy

Create a Wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Prepare  ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $FRED. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap.org Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $FRED token address into Pancakeswap, select $FRED, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap ETH to $FRED

Swap ETH for $FRED and adjust the transaction slippage to 0%.
